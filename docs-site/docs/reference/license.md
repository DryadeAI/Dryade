# Dryade Software Use License (DSUL)

Dryade is licensed under the **Dryade Software Use License (DSUL)**. This is not an open-source license. It is a proprietary license designed to give you freedom to use, deploy, and extend Dryade while protecting the project from unauthorized redistribution and competing hosted services.

## What You Can Do

- **Self-host Dryade** on your own infrastructure -- no restrictions on where or how you deploy it
- **Extend with plugins** -- build custom plugins for your organization's needs
- **Build custom integrations** -- connect Dryade to your existing tools and services via the API
- **Use the API** for your own applications and internal tools
- **Modify the source code** for your own use within your organization
- **Use for free** -- the community edition includes core features at no cost

## What Requires a Commercial License

The following activities require a commercial license agreement with Dryade:

- **Redistribution** -- distributing Dryade or derivative works as your own product
- **Branding removal** -- removing or modifying Dryade branding in distributed versions
- **Hosted service** -- offering Dryade as a managed or hosted service to third parties
- **Enterprise features** -- using Team or Enterprise tier plugins in production requires the corresponding subscription

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use Dryade at my company for free?

Yes. You can self-host the community edition at your organization without charge. The community edition includes the core platform, chat, agents, workflows, knowledge management, and MCP server integration.

### Can I modify the source code?

Yes, for your own organization's use. You can customize Dryade to fit your workflows, add internal integrations, and modify the UI. You may not redistribute modified versions.

### Can I build and sell plugins?

Yes. Plugins you develop are your own intellectual property. You can distribute and sell your plugins through the Dryade marketplace or independently.

### What is the difference between community and paid tiers?

The community edition provides the core platform. Paid tiers (Starter, Team, Enterprise) unlock additional plugins, higher usage limits, and priority support. See [Plans & Features](/reference/tiers) for a detailed comparison.

### Can I use Dryade to build a competing product?

No. The DSUL prohibits using Dryade as the basis for a competing AI orchestration platform or hosted service. You are free to build applications _on top of_ Dryade for your own use or for your customers.

### Is this the same as BSL or SSPL?

No. The DSUL is its own license with different terms. It is more permissive than SSPL for self-hosting use cases, while maintaining restrictions on redistribution and competing hosted services.

### Where can I read the full license text?

The complete license is available in the [LICENSE](https://github.com/DryadeAI/Dryade/blob/main/LICENSE) file in the repository.
